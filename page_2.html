My Story: From Parent to Founder

Family in IT

As a family with 15 years in IT, we value developing analytical, logical skills, and critical thinking in children.

Search for Meaningful STEM

As parents and consumers of various STEM programs, we discovered significant gaps in elementary education.

Program Limitations

Existing programs lacked clear visibility into progress, tangible results, and reinforced fundamental engineering principles.

Creating Inventorium

We developed Inventorium London to provide elementary-aged children with robust, engaging STEM education.

Educational Journey

Our son progressed from Lego inventors club to Scratch to Robocode in Ukraine, and then Code Ninjas in Canada, highlighting the need for more structured hands-on learning.

Community Impact

Inventorium addresses the broader market problem of limited hands-on STEM education for elementary children.
